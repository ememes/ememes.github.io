Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RetroWidgets handles information when you use the app and its widgets. RetroWidgets is designed with a privacy-first, offline-first approach.

1) Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data through servers, accounts, analytics trackers, or ad networks.

  • No account registration
  • No cloud profile
  • No third-party ads
  • No tracking identifiers for advertising

2) Data Used on Device

RetroWidgets uses local device information strictly to render widgets and app previews.

  • Date and time (for clock/calendar widgets)
  • Battery state (for battery-themed widgets)
  • Your customization preferences (stored locally)
Important: These values are processed on-device and are not sent to external servers.

3) Local Storage

RetroWidgets stores your widget configuration locally using iOS app storage / App Group shared storage so the main app and widget extension can stay in sync.

4) Network Usage

RetroWidgets is intended to work fully offline for its core features and does not require external API calls to render widgets.

5) Third-Party Services

RetroWidgets does not embed third-party advertising SDKs or external analytics SDKs in normal use.

6) Children’s Privacy

RetroWidgets does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7) Your Rights

Because data is stored locally on your device, you can remove app data at any time by deleting the app or resetting app content on your device.

8) Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
