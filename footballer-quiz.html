Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 13, 2025
App: Guess the Footballer

This Privacy Policy explains how Guess the Footballer (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

Summary

  • We use Google AdMob to display advertisements.
  • We may use Firebase services (such as Analytics and Crash reporting) to understand app performance and improve stability.
  • The App does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, or phone number.

What “Collect” Means

“Collect” means transmitting data off the device in a way that allows us and/or our third-party partners to access it for longer than necessary to service the request in real time (for example, for analytics, advertising, or diagnostics).

Information We Collect

The App itself does not ask you to provide personal information. However, information may be collected automatically through third-party SDKs integrated into the App.

Automatically Collected Data (via third-party SDKs)

Depending on your device settings and consent, the following types of data may be collected:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising Identifier/IDFA, IDFV) for advertising, fraud prevention, and measurement.
  • Usage data (e.g., app interactions, session duration, screens viewed, gameplay events) for analytics and product improvement.
  • Diagnostics (e.g., crash logs, performance metrics) to improve reliability and fix bugs.
  • Approximate location (in some cases, inferred from IP address for ad delivery—typically at country/region level).

Tracking & Personalized Advertising (Apple ATT)

We may request permission via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework to use your device’s advertising identifier for personalized ads and ad measurement.
